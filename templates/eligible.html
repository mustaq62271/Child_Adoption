Who Can Adopt?

You must be a Bangladesh Citizen.

If you are unmarried, you must be at least 28 years old.

If you are married, you must jointly adopt the child (even if you are separated but not divorced), and your spouse must also be either a Bangladeshi citizen or in legal status in the Bangladesh.

You must meet certain requirements that will determine your suitability as a prospective adoptive parent, including criminal background checks, fingerprinting, and a home study.
